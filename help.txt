PDB Analyzer

It is a Streamlit-based web application for analyzing pdb files, exploring interface metrics, and visualizing 3D protein structures.

Step 1: Define the target and binder chains. 

Step 2: Clarify the residue numbering offset between the initial target PDB and the final output. 

Step 3: Upload the PDB files.

 After a while, the app provides four tabs: 

1. Summary:
 - Displays all analysed results in an interactive table.  

2. Visualisations: 
 - Interactive 3D structure viewer using py3Dmol. 
 - Customizable chain colouring. 
 - Counts and plots the number of 3 Å and 4 Å contacts between binder and target. 

3. Details: 
 - For each design, you can inspect: 
      • Target and binder sequences
      • Residue–residue contact pairs (3 Å and 4 Å) and distance
